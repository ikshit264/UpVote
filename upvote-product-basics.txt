1. Product Basics 
Name: UpVote 
Tagline: Build What Matters 
One-Line Description: A customer feedback and prioritization platform that helps product teams collect, analyze, and execute on user feedback with an embeddable voting widget. 
Category: Feedback Management / Product Prioritization Platform 
Website: upvote.entrext.in 

2. Brand 
Logo Usage: Modern, clean, and community-driven—designed to feel accessible, efficient, and transparent 
3-Adjective Brand Voice: Community-led · Structured · Actionable 
Colors / Style: Professional monochrome, high-contrast typography, vibrant accent charts; clean, focused, and data-driven 

3. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) 
Role / Industry: Product managers, startup founders, SaaS companies, and software developers 
Company Size: 1–500 employees 
Primary Trigger Event: Overwhelmed by fragmented feedback or building features without user validation 

4. Problem & Value 
Core Problem: Product teams waste time and resources building features that users don't actually want 
Solution Gap: Fragmented feedback across Slack, email, and DMs with no clear way to quantify demand 
User Outcome: A data-backed roadmap, increased user retention, and transparent development cycles 

5. Why This, Why Now 
Product Purpose: Make product roadmaps democratic, transparent, and driven by real user data 
Market Shift: Rise of user-centric SaaS development where community buy-in is a competitive necessity 

6. Differentiation 
Key Differentiator: Lightweight, universal embeddable widget that lives directly within your application 
Defensible Advantage: Frictionless integration and outcome-focused workflows that turn raw feedback into prioritized tasks 

7. Features 
Universal Embeddable Widget → Collect feedback directly on your site with one line of code 
Feature Voting System → Crowd-sourced prioritization to identify high-impact requests 
Status Tracking & Roadmap → Inform users of progress from "Planned" to "Done" 
Multi-App Dashboard → Centralized interface to manage feedback for all your products 
Cross-Framework Integration → Seamless support for React, Vue, Angular, and Vanilla HTML 

8. Pricing 
Pricing Model: Subscription-based SaaS 
Plans + Intended Audience: 
Hobby (Solo makers & side projects) · Pro (Growing product teams) · Enterprise (Large-scale operations)

• Early-stage founder validating a product roadmap before building 
• SaaS team quantifying feature demand to reduce development waste 
• Open-source maintainer organizing community requests transparently
